```

```

- Name: Your name
- Email: Your email
- Company: KPRFlow Enterprise (optional)

- Team size: 1 (individual developer)

#### 4. **Submit & Wait**
```
✅ Submit application
⏳ Wait for approval (usually 1-2 days)
📧 Check email for approval notification
🔗 Follow approval link when received
```
